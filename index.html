About

What we do

We are a park sharing and management service. We provide a new and innovative way for parking lot owners and/or anyone with their own driveways to advertise spots for rent on a one-off and on a regular basis. This creates cheap and alternative solutions for users to find temporary parking with the app.

What we offer

We offer two unique synergetic services. The first is a management tool that allows anyone who owns parking spots to provide the spots for rent and manage their availability. The second is a mobile app that displays these parking spots at nearby destinations ideal for anyone who wishes to reserve a location before they reach their destination.

Who we are

Roundaway is mobile community, and a member of the ‘sharing economy’. We strive to ease aspects of the commute, specifically revolved around parking. We believe parking should be affordable simple, and straight forward.
